# Case: misleading low-volume spike

![Misleading-volume graph](../assets/misleading-volume.svg)

## What to notice

- Most buckets have more than 1,100 documents.
- The dramatic spike bucket has only 18 documents.
- The signal returns to baseline after the low-volume bucket.

## Safe interpretation

> The April bucket spikes toward doom, but it is based on only 18 documents, so it is weak evidence and should not be quoted without the volume caveat.

## Unsafe interpretation

> AI suddenly became doom-coded in April.

Why unsafe:

- the spike is the lowest-volume bucket
- visual size is not evidentiary strength
- neighboring high-volume buckets do not support a lasting shift

## Teaching use

Use this to teach document-count bars and screenshot-safe caveats.
